About the club!

Founded in 1948, URI's Outing Club is a Student Senate-recognized organization that aims to provide members with an opportunity to learn and explore nature and the outdoors. We host a variety of trips and activities throughout the school year, including hiking trips, camping, rock climbing, skiing, and more!
