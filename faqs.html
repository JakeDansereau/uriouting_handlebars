Frequently Asked Questions



When does Outing Club Meet?

Outing Club meets every Wednesday at 7:00 PM in Pastore Hall Room 124! If you can't make the meetings, don't worry! We post a meeting recap following every meeting that includes links to sign up to the upcoming outings!


What kinds of 'outings' does Outing Club do?

Outing Club does a wide variety of activities and trips! We take trips to Rockspot, day hikes to local trails, and campfire outings. Some of our bigger trips include weekend getaways for ski trips, weekend hiking trips in New Hampshire, and more!


Does Outing Club cost money to join?

Outing Club is $25 to join, however if you want to come and see what the club is all about you can go on the first outing for free! The $25 fee covers trips to rockspot and gas money for local hikes.
